## Funding Round Proposal

Proposal to open a new funding round.

In the last round before QVAULT v2, held on March 11, the 100,000 QCAP available were fully sold out immediately, in the very first request.

This new round aims to satisfy the demand from those who were unable to purchase QCAP in the previous round.

### Details

- **Amount:** 100,000 QCAP  
- **Price:** 400,000 Qu per QCAP *(a symbolic increase from the previous round price of 399,000 Qu)*

### Timeline

If this proposal is approved, the funding round will begin at the start of epoch 206 (April 1st, 12:00 UTC).
